About Us

JPS Transport is a leading provider of freight and logistics solutions,
serving customers across Srilanka since 2010. Founded by industry veterans Pasindu and Brothers,
JPS has grown to become one of the most trusted names in transportation.

Our fleet of modern, well-maintained vehicles and our team of experienced,
professional drivers allow us to deliver reliable, on-time service for
shipments of all sizes - from small parcels to full truckloads.
Safety is our top priority, and we maintain rigorous training and compliance programs
to ensure the protection of our cargo and our drivers.

With strategically located terminals and warehouses, and a commitment
to leveraging the latest transportation technologies,
JPS is positioned to meet the evolving logistics needs of our diverse customer base.
We take pride in our reputation for excellence and our focus
on building long-term, collaborative partnerships.
